Saeedeh Barzegar Khalilsaraei

I studied Computer Engineering at the University of Guilan, Rasht, Iran. Then, I pursued a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence at Kharazmi University, Tehran, Iran. Currently, I am affiliated with the Institute of Computer Graphics and Knowledge Visualisation while pursuing a PhD in a joint degree between Graz University of Technology and Nanyang Technological University. My research focuses on providing an accurate and coarse mathematical representation in the 2D/3D domain to improve the computer-aided design (CAD) process. Leveraging geometric deep learning techniques, I am developing models that are both computationally efficient and easy to manipulate, enabling designers to work more intuitively with complex data.
